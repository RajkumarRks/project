REGISTRATION

The SPELLL-2024 conference will be held in the hybrid mode (both physical and on-line). The authors of accepted papers will have to present their papers on-line, or on site in CUTN Campus. The registration fee for main conference will cover the pre-conference workshop and all keynote talks. Currently the only possible mode of payment is NEFT/RTGS for Indian participants and wire transfer for foreign participants. The required bank details are mentioned below:



Bank Details and Registration Details will be updated soon


Guidelines

  • Authors of the selected papers should register for the conference to present the paper.
  • The participant is required to make the NEFT/wire transfer before filling up the registration form.
  • Mention SPELLL 2024 in remarks section while making the payment.
  • Fees once paid can not be refunded.
