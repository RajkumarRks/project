About the Department

      The Computer Science Department at the Central University of Tamil Nadu is a key part of the university's science faculties. Since the 2016-2017 academic year, it has been offering a comprehensive M.Sc. Computer Science program. This two-year, four-semester full-time program is designed to provide students with a robust theoretical understanding, enhanced by extensive practical experiences. The department is dedicated to offering top-tier education in the ever-evolving field of Information Technology, focusing on both technical and practical aspects.


      The vision of the department is to become a world-class leader in disseminating knowledge and conducting advanced research in computer science, adhering to international standards. Its mission is to produce graduates who are not only software experts but also possess significant technical creativity and managerial skills. The department emphasizes delivering high-quality education through innovative and best teaching practices. It aims to equip students with the skills needed to tackle the latest technological challenges in the field of computer science.


      The infrastructure of the department includes state-of-the-art Smart Classrooms and a dedicated Department Library. Additionally, it boasts a well-equipped Computer Lab, providing students with the necessary resources to enhance their learning and practical skills in computer science.
