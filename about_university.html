About The University

     The Central University of Tamil Nadu (CUTN), located in Thiruvarur, is renowned for its academic excellence and innovative approach. Established in 2009 by the Government of India, the university is part of a transformative initiative to increase higher education accessibility, particularly for marginalized communities. It began modestly with just eight students and now stands near the Cauvery River tributary, reflecting significant growth. CUTN comprises 13 Schools and 28 Departments, offering a rich variety of 64 academic programs. These include 29 research programs, 22 specialized programs, and several integrated and undergraduate programs. The university's commitment to comprehensive education is further strengthened through partnerships with prestigious institutions, enhancing its educational reach.


     CUTN is situated in Thiruvarur, a town steeped in the rich cultural heritage of the Chozha dynasty, known for its traditional art, music, and dance. The university's campuses, Neelakudi and Nagakudi, are nestled amidst picturesque surroundings, with the River Vettaaru adding to the serene atmosphere. The Neelakudi campus boasts well-equipped academic halls, libraries, and laboratories, alongside a range of amenities like a health center, sports arenas, and a yoga center. Meanwhile, the Nagakudi campus caters to faculty and staff, offering comfortable living quarters and fostering a close-knit educational community. Both campuses are surrounded by lush paddy fields, providing a tranquil learning environment.


      CUTN is committed to nurturing academic excellence and preserving the region's cultural heritage. It offers a diverse range of academic disciplines, from humanities to sciences and technology to arts, fostering creativity and critical thinking. The university also recognizes the importance of sustainable practices and is actively involved in environmental conservation and rural development initiatives. These efforts demonstrate CUTN's role as a responsible educational institution, committed to inclusivity and community engagement.


      The university serves as a beacon of hope, bridging the gap between aspirations and opportunities, especially for underprivileged sections of society. Its commitment to inclusivity, heritage preservation, and sustainable growth has established CUTN as a revered institution, continually evolving to meet the challenges of modern education while staying true to its cultural roots.
